Analysis

From the onset, we can see a strong relationship between the WJP Rule of Law Index on the Corruption Perception index CPI. Wth everthing else holding constant, an increase of 1 on the Rule of Law Index, would result in a 1.17 increase points in CPI. It is worth mentioning that, through interactions we can see how the Rule of Law Index differs per region. From the model we can conclude that some regions will see a greater impact on their CPI, compared to SubSaharan Africa (MiddleEast and North Africa). Meanwhile other countries see a decrease on their CPI. This could be related to bias related to, how much of an impact would better rule of law would impact in Africa, contraray to Europe, where institutions are more established.
